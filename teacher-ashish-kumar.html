Jatin Thakkar

CHESS

  • Jatin is an international rated chess player with the rating 1701 in Classic chess, 1633 in Rapid chess & 1684 in Blitz chess.
  • He has been in the chess community for over a decade, both as a player and a coach.
  • He has been teaching in Chessbrainz Chess Academy for the past two years. Over the past two years,and gathered the experience by teaching at centers all across Mumbai. and worked with NGOs, schools and some private institutes.
  • Jatin was selected by Mumbai University to represent it in West Zone and Nationals. The team successfully secured second place in All India University Chess Tournament.
  • Captain of K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering chess team.
